Have a training class you want to share with the community. CircleCityCon is looking for trainers to run classes for our event. We are looking for workshops from 2-4 hours long or ones that can be divided into two 4 hour workshops running over two days.

Attendance is included with the ticket price (plus a $5 reservation charge which is donated to charity).

What type of training are we looking for? Anything you think the community would enjoy! Topics from CTF, DFIR and Locksport to Electronic Wearables, 3D Printing, Drone Building, and Giving a Talk. We want some traditional ideas and outside the norm topics. Last year, we had incredible classes on hacking embedded systems, OSINT, introduction to pentesting and Raspberry Pi. Our goal is to expand that number significantly and let the community learn from each other.

    Important Dates
  • November 28, 2014 – First Round Opens
  • March 1, 2015 - First Round Ends
  • March 15, 2015 – Training Notification Letters Sent

Submission Guidelines

All submissions should be submitted in a word document or text file with the following information:

  • Submission Title
  • Trainer Name(s)
  • Trainer Contact Information (this is how we’ll contact you).
  • Training Length
  • An abstract for your Training (200 words or less)
  • A detailed syllabus of the training
  • Training keywords
  • Trainer(s) biography (150 words total for you or the group)
  • Have you taught this class before? Where and when?
  • Shirt Size

If the submission is missing any of the above items, it will not be accepted. CircleCityCon reserves the right to throw out papers in which the author’s personal information is contained in the abstract or plain text file.

We will do our best to accommodate equipment needs, but Authors are responsible for any equipment beyond the Internet access and A/V equipment provided.

As compensation, you will be given two tickets to CircleCityCon for 2015. If we have the budget, additional perks will be added.
